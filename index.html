GenAI Directors

Digital Transformation & Strategy

I started GenAI Directors after discovering a gap in the marketplace for business consulting services within SMBs. Historically, businesses would outsource Software/CRM Development, Digital Marketing, and Information Security to three different companies. While that may still be the case for many companies in the future, a major shift is happening.

New AI job roles have been created within enterprise companies, with consulting firms like Accenture, BCG, and the Big 4 leading digital transformation projects. These initiatives create strategy roadmaps for companies to leverage GenAI in their business operations.

GenAI Directors and Chief AI Officers do not come from a specific background; however, many in IT, Sales, and Communications are assuming these roles. GenAI Creative Director positions are now being created at large marketing firms. I identified this trend early after seeing Chase Bank create a position for a GenAI Director in mid-2025.

I secured the domains genaidirectors.com and genaicreativedirector.com. I spent the second half of the year researching this new emerging job role and the approaches consulting firms take for digital transformation strategy roadmaps. Leveraging my network of talented Software Architects, Digital Marketers, and Security Professionals, I created a methodology to complete high-level creative strategy roadmaps for SMBs.

I am not sure where the direction of GenAIDirectors.com will go in the future. However, for now, a resource-based website is coming soon, featuring blogs and eBooks.

As of mid-January 2026, I am accepting clients for digital transformation strategy roadmaps. The goal is to maximize RevOps using new GenAI and Agentic AI that can be leveraged into your current tech stack.

We provide you with a strategy roadmap including a well-thought-out review and plan for the future, backed by me and my internal team of contractors and consulting firms.
